About Us

Galway Sights and Bites started as a website made by four GMIT students, Eoghan, Emma, Matthew and David, with the goal of showing off our beloved city.

Our Mission

As natives of Galway we often found ourselves instinctively going to the same spots that we went to as children, our goal is to help people visting Galway to learn about these same locations so they can get a genuine experience of Galway.
